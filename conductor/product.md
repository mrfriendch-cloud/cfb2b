# Product Definition - B2B Product Exhibition Website

## Overview
A professional B2B product exhibition website designed to showcase products to wholesalers, industrial buyers, and retail partners. The platform serves as a lead generation engine, a comprehensive information hub, and a tool for building brand awareness.

## Target Audience
- **B2B Wholesalers:** Bulk purchasers looking for reliable suppliers.
- **Industrial Buyers:** Businesses seeking detailed technical specifications and product catalogs.
- **Retail Partners:** Businesses sourcing products for retail distribution.

## Core Objectives
- **Lead Generation:** Convert website visitors into direct business inquiries.
- **Information Hub:** Provide a centralized, detailed catalog for existing and potential clients.
- **Brand Awareness:** Establish a professional and trustworthy online presence for the B2B brand.

## Key Features
- **Inquiry System:** Enable users to submit inquiries for specific products directly through the website.
- **Catalog Management:** Robust CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations for managing product listings and categories.
- **Admin RBAC:** A secure admin backend with role-based access control to manage products, inquiries, and settings.
- **ImageKit Integration:** Integrated ImageKit.io for efficient image management, real-time optimization, and global delivery.

## Design Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist & Professional.
- **Focus:** Clean, trustworthy, and focused on product clarity to facilitate professional decision-making.

## Success Metrics
- Increase in the number of high-quality business inquiries.
- Positive feedback from wholesale and industrial partners on catalog usability.
- Improved brand recognition in the target B2B sectors.
